# Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how crossword.network uses cookies.

## What cookies are used for on crossword.network

We use cookies for:

- **Necessary cookies**: to keep you signed in and protect your session
- **Preference cookies** (optional): to remember settings like theme and solver preferences

We do not use advertising cookies on crossword.network.

## Cookie categories

### Necessary cookies (always on)

Necessary cookies support core features like:

- authentication (staying signed in)
- session integrity and security
- protecting forms from abuse

If you block necessary cookies, sign-in and certain features may not work.

### Preference cookies (optional)

Preference cookies help the site remember what you chose so you do not need to set it again.

On crossword.network, preference cookies may include:

- theme (light/dark/system)
- solver UI preferences (such as ECW classic/premium mode)

You can enable or disable preference cookies from the cookie banner.

## Cookies used by crossword.network

Cookie names can vary based on environment and security settings, but these are the ones you may see.

### Authentication cookies (Necessary)

crossword.network uses an authentication system that may set cookies such as:

- `next-auth.session-token` or `__Secure-next-auth.session-token`
- `next-auth.csrf-token`

These cookies are required for sign-in and session security.

### Site cookies (Necessary / Preferences)

| Cookie | Category | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `cw_cookie_consent` | Necessary | Stores your cookie consent choices. |
| `cw_guest_id` | Necessary | Identifies your browser when you play without signing in (for example, to enforce hint limits and prevent abuse). |
| `cw_theme` | Preferences | Remembers your theme preference (light/dark/system). |
| `cw_ecw_mode` | Preferences | Remembers your ECW solver mode (classic/premium). |

## How to change your cookie choices

Use the **Manage** button in the cookie banner to change your preference cookie settings.
